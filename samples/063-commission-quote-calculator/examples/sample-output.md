# Commission Quote Calculator — Example Output

> This is what the agent team produces when you run this sample.

## Scenario

Alexis Rivera is a freelance digital illustrator with 3 years of professional experience. A gaming company reached out requesting a custom character illustration for their new card game: full-body character design, detailed background, commercial use rights, delivered in 3 weeks. Alexis needs to calculate a professional quote that covers her time, ensures fair compensation for her skill level, accounts for commercial usage, and protects her with clear terms.

## What the Agents Did

### Round 1: First Draft

**Pricer** produced:

*Commission Quote: Character Illustration*

Full-body character with background: $800
Timeline: 3 weeks
Payment: 50% deposit, 50% on completion

Total: $800

**TimeEstimator** scored it **65/100** and found:

- No itemized breakdown — client can't see where costs come from
- Hourly rate not specified or calculated against industry standards
- Missing material costs (software subscriptions, hardware wear)
- No mention of commercial use pricing (this should cost significantly more than personal use)
- Revision policy not defined — unlimited revisions could eat profit
- Missing overhead calculation (taxes, insurance, equipment)
- No rush fee despite tight 3-week timeline
- Payment terms too vague (no mention of payment methods, late fees, deposit non-refundability)
- Zero information about what happens if client changes scope mid-project

### Round 2: Improved Draft

**ValueCommunicator** addressed the feedback:

*Completely restructured quote with professional presentation. Calculated Alexis's target hourly rate based on her experience level ($45/hour for 3 years experience in digital illustration market). Broke down project phases: initial concept sketches (6 hours), client feedback round (2 hours), detailed line art (10 hours), coloring and rendering (12 hours), background creation (8 hours), revisions (4 hours), final delivery prep (2 hours) = 44 hours total. Added material costs for Adobe Creative Cloud subscription allocation ($15), Clip Studio Paint ($5), hardware/equipment depreciation ($25). Calculated overhead at 25% ($495 for taxes, insurance, marketing). Applied 50% commercial use multiplier. Included 15% rush fee for 3-week timeline. Detailed revision policy (2 rounds included), clear payment schedule with methods accepted, and cancellation terms.*

**TimeEstimator** scored it **94/100**: "Comprehensive, professional quote that communicates value effectively and protects the artist."

- **Client may NOT:** Resell artwork, create merchandise beyond card game, license to third parties without separate agreement

### PAYMENT TERMS
