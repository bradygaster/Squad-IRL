**What to buy:**
- Bee's Wrap variety pack (7 wraps, various sizes): $35
- *Budget DIY:* Make your own for $12 (see instructions below)

**Where to buy:** Whole Foods (6 Austin locations), Central Market, Amazon, or Austin Zero Waste Store

**Ease:** 🟡 **Moderate** — Requires washing in cold water (no hot water or dishwasher)

**DIY Instructions:**
1. Cut fabric squares from old cotton sheets
2. Melt beeswax + jojoba oil (3:1 ratio)
3. Brush mixture on fabric
4. Bake at 200°F for 5 minutes

## TIPS FOR SUCCESS

✅ **Start small:** Don't try to do everything at once. Phase 1 this month is plenty!

✅ **Keep visible reminders:** Note on door to "grab bags," keep water bottles in car

✅ **Give yourself grace:** Forgot bags? It's okay. Progress, not perfection.

✅ **Track your wins:** Count how many times you use your reusable bottle — feels great to see!

✅ **Make it convenient:** Keep cloth napkins on the table, bags in car trunk, wraps accessible

✅ **Involve your partner:** Shared habits stick better when both people are on board

✅ **Join community:** Austin Zero Waste Living Facebook group for local tips and swap events
