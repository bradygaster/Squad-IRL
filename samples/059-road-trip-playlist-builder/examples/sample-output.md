## Pro Tips

✅ **Download for Offline**: Rural Oregon/Northern CA has spotty service. Download the full playlist.  

✅ **Volume Strategy**: Start quiet (6-7am), louder midday (10am-2pm), back to moderate (evening). Matches energy levels.

✅ **Skip Rule**: Each family member gets 3 "skips" for the whole trip. Use wisely. (Prevents constant song arguments.)

✅ **Sing-Along Rule**: If 3+ people are singing, NO SKIPPING. Democratic joyfulness wins.

✅ **Scenic Pauses**: When you hit a great view (Shasta, Golden Gate Bridge first sighting), pause music for 2-3 minutes. Let the moment breathe.
