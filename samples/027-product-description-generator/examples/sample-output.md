# Product Description Generator — Example Output

> This is what the agent team produces when you run this sample.

## Scenario

Rachel runs a small Etsy shop selling handmade leather laptop bags. Her current product description is functional but boring: "Leather laptop bag, 15 inch, brown, handmade, $185." She's getting views but only 2% convert to sales. She needs SEO-optimized copy that ranks for "leather laptop bag" while also telling a story that makes people click "Add to Cart."

## What the Agents Did

### Round 1: First Draft

**Copywriter** produced:

**Handmade Leather Laptop Bag — 15"**

This beautiful leather laptop bag is perfect for professionals who want style and functionality. Made from high-quality full-grain leather, it features a padded laptop compartment, multiple pockets, and a comfortable shoulder strap.

Our bags are handcrafted with care using traditional techniques. The leather will develop a unique patina over time, making each bag one-of-a-kind. Available in brown, black, and tan.

Price: $185 | Free shipping on orders over $100

**SEOExpert** scored it **71/100** and found:

- Missing key search terms: "leather laptop bag 15 inch," "full grain leather messenger bag," "vintage leather laptop bag" — these are what people actually search for
- No dimensions — customers need to know: Will my MacBook Pro fit? External dimensions? Weight?
- "High-quality full-grain leather" is vague — what kind? (cowhide, vegetable-tanned, etc.) and where sourced?
- "Multiple pockets" — how many, what sizes, what goes in them?
- No social proof — reviews, bestseller status, years in business?
- Missing urgency/scarcity — "handmade" implies limited availability but doesn't say it
- The lifestyle benefit is weak — "professionals who want style" could apply to any bag

### Round 2: Improved Draft

**ConversionOptimizer** added specifics and urgency:

Added exact dimensions (16"W × 12"H × 4"D, fits 13-15" laptops including MacBook Pro 16"), weight (2.4 lbs), and material specifics (vegetable-tanned cowhide from Italian tannery). Detailed pockets: main compartment + 2 interior slip pockets + 1 zippered pocket + 2 exterior pockets. Worked in SEO keywords naturally ("full grain leather messenger bag," "vintage leather laptop bag," "leather bag for MacBook"). Added customer testimonial: "This bag makes me look like I have my life together even when I don't" — Sarah M., graphic designer. Created urgency: "Each bag takes 12 hours to handcraft. Currently 3-4 week lead time due to high demand." Reframed benefit from generic "professionals" to specific use case: "For the creative professional who's tired of looking like everyone else with their nylon laptop backpack."

**SEOExpert** scored it **92/100**: "Strong SEO keyword integration, specific product details, social proof, and conversion-focused urgency. The lifestyle positioning is much clearer."

## THE PATINA PROCESS (It Gets Better with Age)

New leather is beautiful. Aged leather is art.

When you first receive your Montgomery bag, the leather will be smooth and even in color. Over 6-12 months of daily use, it will:
- Darken slightly (especially in high-contact areas)
- Develop natural wear marks and scratches (these are features, not flaws)
- Mold to your body and how you carry it (becomes uniquely yours)
- Gain a soft sheen from natural oils (no leather conditioner needed)

**This is intentional.** Full-grain leather is meant to age. Each bag tells the story of where you've been and what you've done.

**Maintenance:** Wipe with damp cloth occasionally. That's it. No polishing, no conditioning (unless you want to speed up the patina).

## WHY HANDMADE? (And Why It Takes 3-4 Weeks)

We don't mass-produce these bags in a factory. Each Montgomery is handcrafted by one person from start to finish.

**The process:**
1. Cut leather pieces from premium hides (hand-selected for quality)
2. Skive edges (thin them for clean seams)
3. Dye and burnish all edges (smooth, polished finish)
4. Saddle-stitch by hand (12-14 hours of stitching per bag)
5. Attach hardware (riveted, not glued)
6. Condition leather and inspect for quality
7. Package with care instructions

**Current lead time:** 3-4 weeks from order to shipping (we're a small team, orders are filled in sequence)

**Why it's worth the wait:**  
Factory-made bags are cut by machine, assembled in minutes, and quality-checked by algorithm. Ours are made by humans who care whether the stitching is straight. You'll feel the difference the first time you pick it up.
