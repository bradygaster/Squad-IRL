## Visual Organization

**Closet Setup for Easy Mornings**:

1. **Organize by garment type, then by color within type**
   - All tops together (left to right: cream → camel → chocolate → terracotta → olive)
   - All bottoms together (same color order)
   - Dresses separate section
   - Layers/outerwear on separate rod or hooks

2. **Keep Formula Cards in closet**  
   - Print the 7 outfit formulas above on index cards
   - Keep in a small box on your shelf
   - Grab a card when you're uninspired

3. **Shoe Display**
   - 4 pairs fit on a small shelf - keep them visible
   - Seeing them reminds you to use them

4. **One-Week Test**
   - Wear only capsule pieces for one week  
   - Note which combinations you reach for most
   - This will reveal your true style preferences

## Pro Tips

**Maintain the Capsule**:
- ✅ ONE IN, ONE OUT rule: When you buy something new, remove something old
- ✅ Quarterly review: Every 3 months, check for items needing repair/replacement
- ✅ Avoid impulse buys in non-palette colors—they'll become orphans again

**Style Hacks**:
- French tuck everything: Instantly more polished  
- Roll sleeves on blouses/jackets: Adds casual edge to formal pieces
- Half-tuck sweaters: Makes proportions more flattering
- Belt the camel blazer: Instant dress alternative

**Cost per Wear**:
- Your 28-piece capsule creates 52+ outfits
- If you wear each outfit 2x/month = 104 wears/month
- Compare to your old 150-piece closet where you wore 20 items regularly
- You're actually wearing more variety with fewer pieces!
