**1. Answer: (b)** 6COРѓѓ + 6HРѓѓO + light energy Рєњ CРѓєHРѓЂРѓѓOРѓє + 6OРѓѓ

*Explanation: This is the correct balanced equation. (a) is the equation for cellular respiration (backwards). (c) has incorrect water coefficients. (d) is oversimplified and unbalanced.*

**2. Answer: (b)** Chloroplasts

*Explanation: Chloroplasts contain chlorophyll and are the site of photosynthesis. (a) Mitochondria perform cellular respiration. (c) Cell membrane controls what enters/exits. (d) ER makes proteins/lipids.*

**3. Answer: (c)** To release energy from glucose for cell activities

*Explanation: Cellular respiration breaks down glucose to release ATP energy. (a) Oxygen is a byproduct of photosynthesis, not respiration. (b) Describes photosynthesis. (d) Also describes photosynthesis.*

**4. Answer: (d)** Mitochondria

*Explanation: Mitochondria are the "powerhouse" where cellular respiration occurs. All other organelles have different functions.*

**5. Answer: TRUE**

*Explanation: Photosynthesis produces glucose (CРѓєHРѓЂРѓѓOРѓє) and oxygen (OРѓѓ), which are the exact inputs needed for cellular respiration. The two processes are complementary.*

**6. Answers:**
1. Carbon dioxide (COРѓѓ)

**7. Answer: (b)** Thylakoid membranes
