# Influencer Outreach Drafter — Example Output

> This is what the agent team produces when you run this sample.

## Scenario

TechFlow is a startup making a sustainable phone case from ocean plastic. They have $3,000 budget for one micro-influencer partnership. They found Sarah (@minimal.tech.life), a tech lifestyle creator with 47K followers on Instagram who posts iPhone accessories, minimal desk setups, and eco-conscious tech. Her audience is 68% women aged 25-34, engagement rate is 4.2% (excellent). TechFlow needs a compelling pitch that feels personal, not like the 500 other sponsorship emails Sarah gets monthly.

## What the Agents Did

### Round 1: First Draft

**OutreachWriter** produced:

*Subject: Partnership Opportunity*

Hi Sarah,

We love your Instagram account! We're TechFlow and we make phone cases from ocean plastic. We'd love to work with you to promote our product to your followers.

We can offer $500 and free product. Let us know if you're interested!

Thanks,
TechFlow Team

**AuthenticityChecker** scored it **58/100** and found:

- Subject line is generic spam template — likely auto-deleted
- Zero personalization — doesn't reference any specific content Sarah posted
- Doesn't explain WHY TechFlow chose Sarah specifically vs. any other tech influencer
- Compensation ($500 for 47K followers) is very low for deliverables not specified
- No campaign details (what are they asking Sarah to create?)
- Doesn't demonstrate knowledge of Sarah's values or audience
- Tone is transactional, not collaborative
- No mention of creative freedom or brand alignment
- Missing easy "yes" CTA with clear next steps

### Round 2: Improved Draft

**PersonalizationExpert** addressed the feedback:

*Changed subject line to reference Sarah's recent post about reducing tech waste. Opening paragraph references her specific September 12th post about the iPhone case graveyard and her comment about wishing sustainable options existed. Explained why TechFlow aligns with her values (ocean plastic recovery, B-Corp certified). Clearly outlined campaign: 2 Instagram posts, 3 Stories, 1 Reel over 4-week period. Adjusted compensation to $1,200 + 50% affiliate commission (market rate for 47K engaged audience). Emphasized creative freedom and approval process. Included examples of past successful creator partnerships. Added specific CTA with calendar link.*

**AuthenticityChecker** scored it **94/100**: "Feels personal, professional, and respect's creator's value and autonomy."
