# Pet Health Tracker — Example Output

> This is what the agent team produces when you run this sample.

## Scenario

Bella is a 7-year-old golden retriever with a history of ear infections. She's due for her annual vet visit next week. Her owner, Sarah, wants a comprehensive health summary and smart questions to ask the vet.

## What the Agents Did

First draft listed every vet visit since 2018 but didn't highlight patterns or concerning trends. Revised analysis identified: recurring ear infections (4 in past 18 months, all occurring in summer months), weight gain (68 lbs in 2021 → 79 lbs now), and overdue dental cleaning (last one was in 2021). Generated 8 specific questions about chronic ear issues, weight management, and whether senior panel bloodwork is recommended at age 7.

## Final Output

Vet appointment prep sheet with health timeline, red flags highlighted (summer ear infections = likely seasonal allergies, 11 lb weight gain = dietary adjustment needed, 3+ years since dental = tartar buildup risk), current medication list (Apoquel 16mg 2x daily for allergies), upcoming preventive care needs (dental cleaning estimated $450, senior bloodwork panel $180), and 8 tailored questions to ask: "Are Bella's recurring summer ear infections consistent with seasonal allergies? Should we do allergy testing?" "Her weight is up 11 lbs — what's a healthy target weight and feeding plan?" "At age 7, should we start senior bloodwork panels?" **Vet confirmed seasonal allergies, prescribed new diet plan, scheduled dental. Bella's back to healthy weight (70 lbs) 3 months later.**
