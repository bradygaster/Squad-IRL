# Thank You Note Writer — Example Output

> This is what the agent team produces when you run this sample.

## Scenario

Priya and Dev got married 3 weeks ago. They received 47 gifts and need to write personalized thank-you notes before the 3-month deadline. They're exhausted and staring at a blank notecard, unsure how to make each one feel genuine.

## What the Agents Did

First draft was generic: "Thank you for the beautiful gift. We appreciate your generosity." Revised notes are personalized with specific details: Aunt Maya's handmade quilt becomes "Your quilt is already our favorite wedding gift. We've used it for three movie nights this week, and every time we curl up under it, we think of you and your amazing craftsmanship." Uncle James's $200 check becomes "Your generous gift is going directly toward our Italy honeymoon fund. We're putting it toward a cooking class in Tuscany — we'll toast to you with the first glass of Chianti!"

## Final Output

47 unique thank-you notes, each 3-5 sentences, personalized with: specific gift mentioned by name, how they'll use it or what it means to them, genuine gratitude that feels natural, and personal touch referencing the wedding ("Your presence at the ceremony meant the world to us — we'll never forget dancing with you at the reception"). Organized by priority tiers (immediate family first, close friends next, distant relatives last). Includes addressing tips and mailing timeline. **Priya and Dev finished all 47 notes in 2 evenings instead of dreading it for weeks.** No more guilt, only authentic gratitude.
