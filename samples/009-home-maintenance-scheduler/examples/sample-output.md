# Home Maintenance Scheduler — Example Output

> This is what the agent team produces when you run this sample.

## Scenario

Tom and Rachel bought their first home (1,950 sq ft, built 2008) in Phoenix, AZ. They have no idea what maintenance they should be doing or when. Budget: $3,500/year.

## What the Agents Did

First draft missed critical Arizona-specific tasks (monsoon prep, HVAC filters every 30 days due to dust), scheduled roof inspection in July (115°F heat), and lumped all tasks into "Spring." The refined plan includes monthly tasks, seasonal Arizona considerations, DIY vs. pro recommendations, and budget breakdown: $2,847/year with $653 buffer.

## Final Output

12-month calendar with tasks like: January (HVAC service $180, check attic insulation DIY), March (monsoon prep starts, clean gutters $120 or DIY), May (AC tune-up $150 before summer heat), September (re-caulk exterior $200), November (winterize irrigation DIY). Each task includes estimated cost, DIY difficulty (1-5), and pro recommendations. **Protects their $380K investment for $237/month.**
