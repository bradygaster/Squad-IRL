## Common Moving Pitfalls & Solutions

### Pitfall #1: Underestimating packing time
❌ **Mistake**: "We'll pack the week before"  
✅ **Solution**: Start 6 weeks out with non-essentials. Most people need 40-60 hours total packing time.

### Pitfall #2: Not labeling boxes well
❌ **Mistake**: "Kitchen" on every box  
✅ **Solution**: Label with room + contents + priority ("Kitchen - Pots/Pans - Unpack Day 1")

### Pitfall #3: Forgetting to transfer school records early
❌ **Mistake**: Requesting Emma's records a week before enrollment  
✅ **Solution**: Request 6-8 weeks out. Schools are slow, and you need them for new enrollment.

### Pitfall #4: Not booking movers early enough
❌ **Mistake**: Calling movers 2 weeks before June move  
✅ **Solution**: Book 6-8 weeks out. June is PEAK moving season. You'll pay 30-50% more if you wait.

### Pitfall #5: Moving day with a stressed kid
❌ **Mistake**: Emma "helps" on moving day, gets stressed/underfoot  
✅ **Solution**: Emma stays with a friend/family the night before and morning of moving day. Pick her up after movers leave.
