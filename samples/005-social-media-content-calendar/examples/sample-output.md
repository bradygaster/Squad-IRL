# Social Media Content Calendar — Example Output

> This is what the agent team produces when you run this sample.

## Scenario

Sophia runs a sustainable fashion boutique called "ThreadLightly" in Portland. She has 2,400 Instagram followers, 890 Twitter/X followers, and 340 LinkedIn connections. She wants to grow her online presence to drive sales but she's overwhelmed trying to post daily. She needs 30 days of content that educates followers about sustainable fashion, showcases her products, and builds community — all while maintaining her brand voice (eco-conscious, authentic, not preachy).

## What the Agents Did

### Round 1: Initial Calendar

**Creator** generated 30 days of posts across all three platforms (90 posts total).

The content included product features, sustainability tips, and engagement posts, but the mix was off: 40% promotional, 35% educational, 25% community.

**BrandVoice** scored it **74/100** and found:

- Content mix doesn't match spec (should be 40% educational, 30% entertaining, 20% promotional, 10% community)
- Several Instagram captions exceed 2,200 characters (platform limit)
- Twitter/X posts don't account for 280-character limit; 8 posts are too long
- Hashtags are generic (#fashion, #style) instead of niche-specific (#sustainablefashion, #ethicalfashion)
- Missing optimal posting times
- Week 2 has no engagement-driving posts (polls, questions, CTAs)
- Some product photos suggested don't exist in ThreadLightly's actual inventory

### Round 2: Polished Calendar

**Strategist** addressed the feedback:

- Adjusted content mix to match percentages exactly (40% educational, 30% entertaining, 20% promotional, 10% community)
- Trimmed all Instagram captions under 2,200 characters
- Rewrote Twitter/X posts to stay under 280 characters
- Replaced generic hashtags with niche tags (#slowfashion, #ethicalwardrobe, #PDXfashion)
- Added optimal posting times for each platform (based on ThreadLightly's audience analytics)
- Added 3 engagement posts per week (polls on styling, questions about sustainability habits, CTAs to share thrift finds)
- Verified all product mentions against actual inventory

**BrandVoice** scored it **95/100**: "Strong brand voice, proper content mix, platform-optimized. Ready to schedule."
