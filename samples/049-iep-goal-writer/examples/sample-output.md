# IEP Goal Writer — Example Output

> This is what the agent team produces when you run this sample.

## Scenario

Ms. Rodriguez is a special education teacher writing IEP goals for Jayden, a 4th grader with ADHD and a specific learning disability in reading (dyslexia). Jayden is currently reading at a mid-2nd grade level (Fountas & Pinnell Level J, DRA 18). He struggles with decoding multisyllabic words, reading fluency, and reading comprehension. His current IEP goals are vague ("Jayden will improve his reading skills") and got flagged in last year's compliance audit. She needs SMART goals that meet state standards and will actually help Jayden make progress.

## What the Agents Did

### Round 1: First Draft

**SpecialEdExpert** produced:

**Goal 1**: Jayden will improve his reading level by one grade level by the end of the year.

**Goal 2**: Jayden will increase his reading comprehension.

**Goal 3**: Jayden will improve his ability to decode words.

**ComplianceChecker** scored it **62/100** and found:

- Not measurable—"improve" is vague, no baseline data, no specific success criteria
- Not time-bound—"by the end of the year" is too vague (which year? how long?)
- Missing method of measurement—how will you assess progress?
- No conditions specified—in what setting? with what supports?
- Doesn't align with specific curriculum or standards
- "One grade level" is arbitrary and may not be realistic for a student with dyslexia

### Round 2: Improved Draft

**GoalCrafter** addressed the feedback:

Established clear baseline data with specific assessment tools (Fountas & Pinnell, DIBELS, Acadience), created measurable goals with specific success criteria (words correct per minute, accuracy percentages, comprehension question scores), aligned goals with Common Core State Standards for 3rd grade (appropriate intermediate target), specified conditions (with/without supports), included realistic timeframes with progress monitoring schedule, and added accommodation recommendations that support goal attainment.

**ComplianceChecker** scored it **94/100**: "Meets quality threshold. Compliant, measurable, and student-centered."

## Present Levels of Academic Achievement and Functional Performance (PLAAFP) Summary

**Reading Decoding**:  
- **Current Level**: Jayden can decode CVC words and simple CVCE words with 85% accuracy. He struggles with multisyllabic words (3+ syllables), decoding them with 45% accuracy. He frequently skips syllables or adds incorrect syllables.  
- **Assessment Data** (January 2024):
  - DIBELS Nonsense Word Fluency: 28 correct letter sounds/minute (benchmark: 50)  
  - Acadience Oral Reading Fluency: 42 words correct per minute (WCPM) at 2nd grade level (benchmark for 4th grade: 118 WCPM)

**Reading Fluency**:
- **Current Level**: Jayden reads 2nd grade texts at 42 WCPM with 87% accuracy but with frequent self-corrections, repetitions, and choppy prosody. He reads in a word-by-word manner with little expression.  
- **Assessment Data**: DIBELS ORF (2nd grade passage): 42 WCPM, 87% accuracy

**Reading Comprehension**:
- **Current Level**: Jayden can answer literal "right there" comprehension questions with 70% accuracy but struggles with inferential questions (40% accuracy) and cause-effect relationships. He loses track of the main idea in longer passages.  
- **Assessment Data**: Fountas & Pinnell Benchmark (Level J): Comprehension score 4/10 (below proficient)

**Strengths**:  
- Strong verbal communication skills; can retell stories orally when read to him  
- High interest in graphic novels and science topics (space, animals)  
- Responds well to multisensory instruction (Orton-Gillingham methods)  
- Persistent; doesn't give up even when frustrated

**Areas of Need**:  
- Multisyllabic word decoding  
- Reading fluency and automaticity  
- Reading comprehension strategies (especially inference and main idea)

## Goal 1: Decoding Multisyllabic Words

**Measurable Annual Goal**:  
By March 14, 2025, when presented with a list of 20 randomly selected multisyllabic words (3-4 syllables) at the 3rd grade level, Jayden will decode the words with 80% accuracy (16/20 correct) across 4 out of 5 consecutive trials, as measured by teacher-administered word reading assessments.

**Baseline** (March 2024): 45% accuracy (9/20 correct) on 3-4 syllable words

**Alignment**: Common Core State Standard RF.3.3.C (Decode multisyllable words)

**Method of Measurement**:  
- Teacher-created word lists using 3rd grade Fry and Dolch word lists  
- Progress monitoring: Bi-weekly (every other Friday)  
- Assessment tool: CORE Phonics Survey (multisyllabic section)

**Conditions**:  
- Words presented in isolation (not in context)  
- Quiet, small-group setting (resource room)  
- No time limit (untimed assessment to focus on accuracy)

**Supports Provided**:
- Systematic multisensory phonics instruction (Wilson Reading System, Tier 3)  
- Syllable division strategy instruction (VC/CV, V/CV, VC/V patterns)  
- Daily 15-minute decoding practice with manipulatives (syllable tiles)

---

### Short-Term Objectives (Benchmarks)

**Objective 1.1** (by June 2024 - End of 4th Grade):  
When presented with 20 3-syllable words at the late 2nd grade level, Jayden will decode with 70% accuracy (14/20 correct) across 3/5 consecutive trials.

**Objective 1.2** (by November 2024 - Mid-5th Grade):  
When presented with 20 3-syllable words at the early 3rd grade level, Jayden will decode with 75% accuracy (15/20 correct) across 3/5 consecutive trials.

**Objective 1.3** (by March 2025 - End of IEP Year):  
When presented with 20 4-syllable words at the 3rd grade level, Jayden will decode with 80% accuracy (16/20 correct) across 4/5 consecutive trials. (ANNUAL GOAL ACHIEVEMENT)

## Goal 2: Reading Fluency

**Measurable Annual Goal**:

**Baseline** (March 2024): 42 WCPM with 87% accuracy at 2nd grade level (Lexile 320L)

**Alignment**: Common Core State Standard RF.3.4.B (Read grade-level prose and poetry orally with accuracy, appropriate rate, and expression)

**Method of Measurement**:  
- DIBELS 8th Edition ORF (3rd grade benchmark passages)  
- Progress monitoring: Monthly (first Friday of each month)  
- Data tracked on Jayden's fluency graph (student self-monitors progress)

- Small-group setting or 1:1 with teacher

## Goal 3: Reading Comprehension - Inferencing

**Measurable Annual Goal**:  
By March 14, 2025, after reading or listening to a 3rd grade level passage (Lexile 500-700L), Jayden will answer 4 out of 5 inferential comprehension questions correctly (80% accuracy) using text evidence, across 4 out of 5 consecutive weekly assessments.

**Baseline** (March 2024): 40% accuracy (2/5 correct) on inferential questions at 2nd grade level

**Alignment**: Common Core State Standard RL.3.1 (Ask and answer questions to demonstrate understanding, referring explicitly to the text as the basis for the answers)

**Method of Measurement**:  
- Teacher-created comprehension questions aligned with Bloom's Taxonomy (analyzing, inferring)  
- Fountas & Pinnell Comprehension Assessment (inference questions specifically)  
- Progress monitoring: Weekly (every Thursday)

**Conditions**:  
- Passage read independently OR read aloud by teacher (to remove decoding barrier and isolate comprehension skill)  
- Jayden may use graphic organizer (inference equation: text clues + background knowledge = inference)  
- Questions asked orally or in written format (Jayden's choice to accommodate ADHD)

**Supports Provided**:
- Explicit instruction in inferencing strategies ("reading between the lines")  
- Graphic organizers: Inference equation anchor chart, character feeling maps  
- Think-aloud modeling by teacher (shows how proficient readers make inferences)  
- High-interest texts matching Jayden's interests (space, animals, graphic novels)

---

### Short-Term Objectives (Benchmarks)

**Objective 3.1** (by June 2024):  
Jayden will answer 3 out of 5 inferential questions correctly (60% accuracy) at 2nd grade level across 3/5 consecutive weeks.

**Objective 3.2** (by November 2024):  
Jayden will answer 3 out of 5 inferential questions correctly (60% accuracy) at early 3rd grade level across 4/5 consecutive weeks.

**Objective 3.3** (by March 2025):  
Jayden will answer 4 out of 5 inferential questions correctly (80% accuracy) at 3rd grade level across 4/5 consecutive weeks. (ANNUAL GOAL ACHIEVEMENT)

## Goal 4: Reading Comprehension - Main Idea

**Measurable Annual Goal**:  
By March 14, 2025, after reading or listening to a 3rd grade level informational text passage (Lexile 500-700L), Jayden will identify the main idea and 3 supporting details with 80% accuracy (4 out of 5 elements correct) across 4 out of 5 consecutive weekly assessments.

**Baseline** (March 2024): 30% accuracy (identifies main idea only, struggles with supporting details)

**Alignment**: Common Core State Standard RI.3.2 (Determine the main idea of a text; recount the key details and explain how they support the main idea)

**Method of Measurement**:  
- Teacher-created main idea assessments using grade-level informational texts  
- Graphic organizer: Main idea "umbrella" with supporting detail "raindrops"  
- Progress monitoring: Weekly (every Wednesday)

**Conditions**:  
- Passage read aloud by teacher (removes decoding barrier)  
- Jayden completes graphic organizer independently after hearing passage twice  
- 10-minute time limit (accommodates ADHD with structured time)

**Supports Provided**:
- Explicit instruction in main idea vs. topic vs. details  
- Color-coded highlighting (yellow = main idea, green = supporting details)  
- Sentence frames: "The main idea is _____. One detail is _____."  
- High-interest informational texts (National Geographic Kids, Scholastic News)

---

### Short-Term Objectives (Benchmarks)

**Objective 4.1** (by June 2024):  
Jayden will identify the main idea and 1 supporting detail (2/5 elements) at 2nd grade level with 50% accuracy across 3/5 weeks.

**Objective 4.2** (by November 2024):  
Jayden will identify the main idea and 2 supporting details (3/5 elements) at early 3rd grade level with 60% accuracy across 3/5 weeks.

**Objective 4.3** (by March 2025):  
Jayden will identify the main idea and 3 supporting details (4/5 elements) at 3rd grade level with 80% accuracy across 4/5 weeks. (ANNUAL GOAL ACHIEVEMENT)
